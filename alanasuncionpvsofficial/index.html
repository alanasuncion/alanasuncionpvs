About Us

#1 Destination perfection in photo, video, and printing services

Alan Asuncion's entrepreneurial journey was inspired by a deep-seated passion for photography, a desire to create a space where his artistic talent could shine. Fueled by this vision, he founded Alan Asuncion Photo and Video Services, aiming to offer clients not just visual content but an immersive experience of their cherished moments. With an unwavering commitment to excellence, Alan infuses each project with his unique perspective, capturing the essence of every occasion. The result is a business built on the foundation of artistic expression and a dedication to turning fleeting moments into timeless memories.

Services


In addition to his artistic aspirations, Alan Asuncion Photo and Video Services serves as a means for Alan to support his family's everyday financial needs, turning a personal passion into a livelihood that brings both artistic fulfillment and practical support.


He has won countless awards for his astonishing photos in Mega Pixel International and in WPPP or the Wedding and Portrait Photographers of the Philippines.
